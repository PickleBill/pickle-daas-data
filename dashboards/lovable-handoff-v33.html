Build a full-featured data intelligence dashboard app for Pickle DaaS — an AI-powered system that turns pickleball video into structured brand intelligence, player profiles, and narrative content.


What Pickle DaaS Is


An AI pipeline that processes pickleball video clips: detects brands (paddles, shoes, sponsor banners), profiles players (9 skill dimensions), classifies everything into revenue lanes (player equipment vs venue installs vs sponsor signage), and generates ESPN-style narratives. Currently analyzing 3,588 clips with 358 brands detected across 54 human-approved classification rules.


Data Architecture


All data is served as static JSON from GitHub Pages. Base URL:


https://picklebill.github.io/pickle-daas-data/

API Endpoints (fetch these):


|----------|------|------------|

EndpointWhatKey fields
`api/feed.json`Master stats`corpus.total_clips`, `corpus.unique_brands`, `corpus.unique_players`, `top_brands`
`api/brands.json`All brands with analytics`brands[].name`, `.detections`, `.unique_clips`, `.share_of_court`, `.primary_category`, `.avg_skills`
`api/players.json`Player profiles`players[].username`, `.archetype`, `.skills` (9 dimensions), `.primary_brand`, `.avg_quality`
`api/clips.json`Top clips`top_quality[]`, `top_viral[]` — each has `uuid`, `video_url`, `thumbnail`, `summary`, `brands[]`
`api/sponsors.json`Sponsor classification`subtypes` (5 types), `subtype_totals`, each brand has `example_clips`
`api/player-equipment.json`Equipment by category`categories` (paddle, shoes, apparel, etc.), each brand has `market_share_pct`, `example_clips`
`api/venue-tech.json`Venue hardware`broadcast_tech`, `facility_chains`, `venue_installed` — each brand has detections + clips
`api/ideas.json`AI research findings`top_strategic[]`, `top_data[]`, each has `title`, `evidence`, `confidence`, `business_value`
`brand-provenance.json`Classification rules`brands[].source` (rule/fallback), `.rule_version`, `.human_verified`, `.rationale`
`classification-stats.json`System stats`total_brand_detections`, `rule_count`, `coverage_pct`, `by_context_flag`
`brand-co-occurrence.json`Brand relationships`nodes[]` (brands), `edges[]` (co-occurrence pairs with weights)
`corpus-timeline.json`Growth over time`timeline[]` with `date`, `clips`, `unique_brands`, `total_detections`

Design System


  • **Background:** `#08080C` (near-black with blue-purple undertone)
  • **Text:** White at 7 opacity tiers: 0.92, 0.70, 0.50, 0.40, 0.30, 0.25, 0.20
  • **Accent colors:** Green `#6EEFC0`, Blue `#6BA3FF`, Amber `#FFB86C`, Purple `#BD93F9`, Gold `#F1C40F`, Red `#FF6B6B`
  • **Glass cards:** `rgba(255,255,255,0.03)` bg, `0.05` border, `backdrop-filter:blur(12px)`
  • **Fonts:** Manrope (display, weight 800, tracking -0.04em), Red Hat Mono (data labels)
  • **Gradient text:** `-webkit-background-clip:text` with per-section gradient pairs
  • **Animations:** Scroll reveals (translateY 40px → 0), counter animations, hover lift on cards

    - 9-section scrolling narrative with video hero

    - Revenue simulator with sliders

    - AI debate visualization

    - Contact CTA


    4. /equipment — Equipment Performance

    - Category tabs (paddle, shoes, apparel, etc.) from player-equipment.json

    - Market share bars per category

    - Brand cards with skill correlations


    5. /players — Player Intelligence / DNA Lab

    - Interactive radar chart (9 skill axes) with player selection

    - Compare 2-3 players with overlaid radars

    - Brand × Skill cross-tab (interactive table)

    - Player leaderboard sortable by any skill


    6. /venues — Venue Intelligence

    - Venue tech breakdown from venue-tech.json

    - Camera tech, facility chains, court surfaces

    - Install base visualization

    - Browse clips by narrative arc type

    8. /evidence — Evidence Wall

  • Glass morphism on interactive cards
