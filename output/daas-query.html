Are speed-ups an effective shot?
Myth Busted
No. Speed-ups have a 12.5% win rate and a 20.8% error rate in the corpus. The conventional wisdom — "be aggressive at the kitchen" — is statistically wrong at the 3.5–4.0 level. Speed-ups work best as set-up shots, not finishers. Players who speed up to force a pop-up win. Players who speed up to end the rally directly lose.

Which clips go viral — and why?
Product Signal
