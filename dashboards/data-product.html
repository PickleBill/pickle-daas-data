Why This Data Is Defensible

Exclusive Camera Network

Courtana camera infrastructure at venues provides raw video feed. No competitor has this installed base.

Replication: $50M+ to build equivalent camera network | 3-5 years

Proprietary Analysis Schema

134K+ data points with unique 9-dimension skill profiling, shot-by-shot analysis, and brand detection trained on real gameplay.

Replication: 6+ months of ML development + domain expertise | 12-18 months

Compounding Data Flywheel

Every new clip makes the corpus more valuable. Historical data cannot be recreated. Network effects: more venues = more data = better models = more venues.

Replication: Cannot be replicated retroactively | Never (historical data is gone)

Cost Advantage

$0.0015/clip analysis cost via Gemini Flash. Competitors using GPT-4 Vision would pay 50-100x more.

Replication: N/A — architectural advantage | 6 months to match architecture

First-Mover Data Monopoly

No other company has structured pickleball game data at scale. Sports betting, fantasy, and analytics companies have zero pickleball data sources today.

Replication: Requires camera partnerships + ML pipeline | 2-3 years minimum
