Methodology

Impression Model: Multi-channel weighted: venue screen (100 base), social organic (500), social paid (2500), coaching feed (300)

Quality Factor: Clip quality score (1-10) mapped to 0.3-2.0 multiplier

Viral Factor: Viral potential (1-10) mapped to 0.5-3.0 multiplier

Arc Engagement: Story arc type applies engagement multiplier (0.7x routine to 1.8x athletic highlight)

CPM Sources: eMarketer + IAB Sports Media benchmarks, adjusted for niche sports premium
