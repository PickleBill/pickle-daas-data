01 Vision & Business Case

The 30-second pitch

The Problem

Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in the US — 36M+ players, no statcast equivalent. Brands (JOOLA, Selkirk, CRBN) spend on sponsorships with zero data on exposure or user skill profiles. Coaches rely on gut feel. Venues have cameras but no intelligence layer.

The Solution

An AI-powered data-as-a-service pipeline that turns any pickleball video clip into structured intelligence: shot breakdown, skill profiles across 9 dimensions, brand detection, badge predictions, coaching commentary — for $0.0054 per clip.

Why Now

Gemini 2.5 Flash gives us human-level video understanding at penny-scale cost. Courtana's 4,097+ clip corpus is the proving ground. We're the only team with hardware, brand relationships, and AI pipeline in one stack.

Proof Points (Today)

  • — clips analyzed end-to-end
  • — total pipeline spend
  • 37.9% badge precision, 49% recall against Courtana ground truth
  • — dominates brand detection — measurable at venue level
  • Pipeline runs hourly, unattended
