This explains why grind rally is overdetected in arcs but undetected in badges—model lacks shot-type recognition. **Business value:** HIGH **Relevant to:** coaching, media_broadcast **Action:** Separate badge classifier into two pipelines: (1) achievement-based (levels, milestones) using rule-based logic; (2) shot/action-based using video frame analysis with temporal convolution; retrain action pipeline on manually annotated shot sequences. --- ### 3.
