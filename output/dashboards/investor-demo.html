Why this matters

Three things make the data layer defensible.
01

Booking-agnostic

Courtana layers on top of any reservation system. Venues don't have to switch — they just plug in cameras and start receiving intelligence.

02

Data flywheel as moat

Every clip improves the next analysis. Brand detection, badge prediction, skill ratings — all calibrate against ground truth. Competitors start at zero; we already have 510.

03

Multi-sided market

Venues pay for retention. Brands pay for intelligence (JOOLA alone shows up in 323 clips). Coaches pay for personalized feedback. Players pay for highlights and gamification.
