EXPERIMENT DESIGN: Crowdsourced Coaching Bounty Board MVP

Hypothesis

Coaches will actively bid on and complete video analysis tasks when offered gamified rewards (XP + badges) tied to a visible credibility score, generating sufficient submission volume and quality feedback to validate the crowdsourcing flywheel.

---

Success Metric

Primary: 5+ coaches submit 2+ analyses each within 24 hours, with average feedback quality rated ≥3.5/5 by independent rater. Secondary: ≥1 coach explicitly states they'd return to complete more bounties if reward structure existed.

---

Build Scope (30-Hour Window)

What You're Building

A single-page web form + manual admin dashboard (not a full platform):

1. Coach Landing Page (3 hours)

  • Displays 1 anonymized pickleball clip (30 seconds max)
  • Shows: "Bounty: Analyze this rally. Submit analysis below."
  • Input field for coach feedback (text, max 500 words)
  • Displays mock reward: "+10 XP, +1 Analysis Badge"
  • One-click submit

2. Clip + Instructions (2 hours)

  • Pull 1 representative rally from Courtana corpus
  • Write 2-sentence coaching prompt: "What's this player doing wrong? What should they fix?"
  • Host on simple CDN or embedded video

3. Manual Scoring Admin Panel (3 hours)

  • Google Sheet or Airtable with columns: Coach Name | Submission | Quality Score (1-5) | Timestamp
  • You manually rate each submission
  • Export results

4. Recruitment (2 hours)

  • Email/Slack blast to 10–15 existing Courtana users who've mentioned coaching interest
  • Message: "We're testing a new way coaches can earn XP and badges. Try it here: [link]. Takes 5 min."
  • No signup required; use email as identifier

5. Buffer/Polish (20 hours)

---

What to Do Monday Morning

1. Identify 1 rally clip from Courtana corpus: intermediate-level play, visible coaching opportunity.

2. Write the bounty prompt (2 sentences max).

3. Hand brief to dev: "Build single-page form, no auth, outputs to Google Sheet."

4. List 15 coach prospects (past players, local pros, Courtana power users).

5. Send blast by 11am: "New experiment: analyze 1 clip, earn 10 XP. 5 min task. Help us test this."

---

Reward Strategy (What You Show Coaches)

  • Display (not paid yet): "+10 XP, +1 Coaching Badge, +0.1 Credibility Score"
  • Copy: "Top-rated analyses earn coach credits toward Courtana Pro features."
  • Don't promise money today. Just signal it exists. Coaches' intrinsic motivation to build portfolio + visible XP/badge is enough for this sprint.

---

Success Looks Like

  • 5+ coaches submit (not 1, not 2)
  • Quality spreads evenly (no single outlier skewing results)
  • At least 1 coach replies to your follow-up with "I'd do this again if [condition]"
  • Feedback is actionable (not generic; addresses specific technique, positioning, etc.)

---

What Happens Next (Win Scenario)

If ≥5 coaches engage with 3.5+ avg quality:

  • Phase 2 (48–72 hours): 10 clips, token reward system (Courtana XP → redeemable for 1-month Pro discount or 30 min coaching session credit)
  • Phase 3 (1 week): Launch credibility scoring algorithm (auto-grade feedback by correlation to player improvement data)
  • Phase 4 (2 weeks): Partner with 1 local venue for lesson-credit rewards + brand sponsorship pilot
