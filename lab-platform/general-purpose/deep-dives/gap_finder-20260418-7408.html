Evidence

The corpus captures what players DO (shots, badges) but not WHO is doing it. Without skill stratification, you cannot determine if 'dink' dominance reflects beginner patterns vs. pro strategy, or whether viral videos skew toward entertaining mistakes or elite play. Evidence: 4283 videos with 'dominant_shot' and 'badges' but zero fields for player level, rating, or experience tier. courtana_participants captured by username but no skill metadata.

Currently assumes skill-blindness is fatal without proving impact.

Blind spot: Conflates 'not labeled' with 'unknowable.' Players may be stratified by implicit signals (shot diversity, consistency, opponent caliber). Also: high-skill vs. low-skill players may have SIMILAR dink patterns—the gap might not matter. No hypothesis stated.
