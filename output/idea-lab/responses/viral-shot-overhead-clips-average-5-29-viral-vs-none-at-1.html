Why This Matters

1. Beginner content = growth fuel. Your fastest-growing user cohort (beginners) responds hardest to overhead clips. They're learning and hungry for "wow" moments.

2. Different motivations. Advanced players already hit overheads; they want strategy or match drama. Beginners want proof it's possible + technique.

3. Flywheel opportunity. Beginner-targeted overhead content drives engagement, which surfaces more to beginners, which recruits new beginners.
