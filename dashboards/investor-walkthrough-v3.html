Classification taxonomy

Four buckets.
Every brand assigned.

The taxonomy engine routes each brand detection into one of four contexts — each with distinct revenue implications for venues and brands.

Player Equipment
—
Gear worn or carried by players. Highest brand equity signal — real purchasing decisions, not venue contracts.
Venue Installed
—
Infrastructure on-court: net systems, cameras, surfaces. Venue procurement, not player choice.
Sponsor Signage
—
Brands advertising at venues: banners, court branding, event logos. Direct monetization path.
Broadcast Tech
—
Cameras, streaming hardware, overlay watermarks. The tech that makes data collection possible.
