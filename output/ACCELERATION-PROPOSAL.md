## What I Just Saw in the App

Bill is **#1 Global**, Level 19 Gold III, 349,350 XP, 95 avatars, ∞ AI credits.

The app currently has:
- **Highlights** — list of clips, manual AI Analyze button, badges awarded per clip
- **AI Coaching** — player manually uploads a video → gets AI coaching feedback
- **Profile** — Rank, XP, Level, Avatars. Pure gamification, zero shot data.

**What's missing:** The DaaS layer. There is zero shot intelligence, zero play style data, zero coaching metrics visible anywhere in the app. The AI Analyze button is there — but it only works on manual uploads, and the output doesn't persist as part of a player profile.

The pipeline we built today is the backend that fills that gap. The question is how we surface it.

## The Core Reframe

**Current model:** Player plays → gets a clip → optionally clicks AI Analyze → gets one-time feedback → forgotten.

**What it should be:** Player plays → every clip auto-analyzed → data feeds into a living player profile → coaching signals compound over time → player improves → comes back more → venue gets more data → flywheel spins.

The difference between these two models is approximately: the difference between a scoreboard and Sportradar.

## 5 Layout Changes That Would Make This Thing Fly

### 1. Add a "Player DNA" Tab to the Profile Page

**Right now:** Profile = Rank / XP / Level / Avatars. That's it. No shot data, no tendencies, no coaching signals.

**What to add:** A "My Game" tab on the profile page with:
- **Kitchen Score** (0–10, derived from session highlights) — the single number that matters
- **Play Style** — your archetype tag(s): "Kitchen Specialist · Patient Player"
- **DUPR Estimate** — AI-estimated from your video, auto-updated
- **Shot Mix** — mini donut chart: Dink 64% / Drive 23% / Speed-Up 3%
- **Signature Moves** — what shot sequences appear repeatedly in your clips
- **Improvement Trend** — Kitchen Score over last 30 days

This data is all already generated by the pipeline. It just needs a UI surface.

**Effort:** Medium — all data exists in `output/batches/` JSONs. Need API endpoint + Lovable component.

---

### 2. Replace Clip Cards with Intelligence Cards

**Right now:** Highlights list = timestamp + player count + badge count. That's it.

**What to add:** Each clip card shows:
- **Wow Score** badge (1–10, already generated)
- **Best shot type** detected in clip ("12-shot dink battle → kitchen winner")
- **DUPR tier** of players visible
- **Coaching tag** ("Teaching Moment" / "Signature Move" / "Viral Moment")
- One-tap to see shot-by-shot breakdown

This makes every highlight feel like ESPN, not a raw video file.

**Effort:** Low — analysis JSON already generated per clip. Map to card component.

---

### 3. "Court TV" Mode in Live Feeds

**Right now:** Facility Staff → Live Feeds = operational camera view.

**What to add:** A TV-overlay mode that shows, in real time as clips come in:
- Shot type detected: **DINK** → **SPEED UP** → **WINNER**
- Current rally length counter
- Kitchen control percentage for this rally
- Wow factor score as it builds

The `output/broadcast/broadcast-dashboard.html` already exists. It needs to be wired to real incoming clip data.

**Why it matters:** This is the demo moment. You put it on the TV at the venue during peak hours. Every player sees their shots being tracked in real time. That's the thing that makes someone pull out their phone and sign up.

**Effort:** Medium — broadcast HTML exists, needs real-time data feed from clip analysis.

---

### 4. Venue Intelligence Panel (Facility Staff → Data)

**Right now:** Facility Staff has operational stuff — Courts, Sessions, Bluetooth, Video Ports. No analytics.

**What to add:** A "Data Intelligence" section with:
- **Today's Court Activity** — sessions by court, peak hours heatmap
- **Player Skill Distribution** — DUPR breakdown of today's players
- **Shot Mix Across Venue** — what your players are actually doing
- **Top Highlights Today** — highest wow-factor clips ready to share
- **Sponsor Exposure** — if brand detection finds JOOLA/Selkirk on paddles, log it
- **Week-over-week retention** — are the same players coming back?

This is the thing a venue operator looks at on an iPad while standing at the front desk. It answers: "Is this system worth what I'm paying for it?"

**Effort:** Medium-High — needs aggregate pipeline output wired to a venue-specific dashboard.

---

### 5. The Shareable "Game Report" — Post-Session

**Right now:** After playing, players get clips. That's it.

**What to add:** A post-session Game Report, auto-generated 30 min after a session ends:
- "You played 47 shots this session. 71% dinks. Kitchen Score: 7.4"
- "Your best rally: [embedded clip], 18 shots, wow factor 8"
- "Compared to last week: Kitchen Score ↑ 0.6, Speed-up win rate ↓ (still premature — avoid before 4+ dinks)"
- "Badge earned: Patient Dinkmaster 🏅"
- One-tap share to Instagram/WhatsApp

This is the thing that brings players back. Not the clip — the *analysis*. "I improved my Kitchen Score. I want to keep improving."

**Effort:** High — needs session attribution, aggregate per-player stats, templated report generator. But this is the product.

## How to Go Faster (The Iterative Model)

Stop thinking about "overnight batch runs." Start thinking about this as three loops running continuously:

**Loop 1: Clip Analysis (every 15 min)**
- Cron job checks for new clips on the anon endpoint
- Auto-analyzes any new clips not in the corpus
- Appends to `enriched-corpus.json`
- $0.0054 per clip, ~$1–2/day at current volume
- Use `/schedule` or a simple cron → can set this up right now

**Loop 2: Profile Refresh (every session)**
- After a player's session ends, re-run player DNA aggregation for their clips
- Update their Kitchen Score, play style tags, DUPR estimate
- Write to a player-specific JSON keyed on username
- Push to gh-pages or Supabase (when deployed)

**Loop 3: Venue Intelligence (daily)**
- Aggregate all clips from last 24h by venue
- Update venue-level stats: player skill distribution, shot mix, top highlights
- Generate a daily brief (already templated — MORNING-BRIEF.md format)
- Post to Slack (or email to Bill)

These three loops together = a real-time intelligence system. No overnight runs. No manual kick-offs. Just continuous data.
